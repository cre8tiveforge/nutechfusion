## Project 2: Church Expansion VR Visualization

**Category:** VR Visualization / Architectural

**Client:** Church Community (Minister-led expansion project)

**Challenge:** A church wanted to expand by building an assisted living facility on their property. The community had concerns about how the new building would look in the neighborhood and its visual impact.

**Solution:** Built a comprehensive VR visualization showing a 1:1 scale representation of:
- The existing church building
- The proposed assisted living facility
- Surrounding neighborhood houses
- Accurate spatial relationships and visual impact

**Technologies:**
- Unity (VR Development)
- Blender (3D Modeling)
- VR Headsets (Oculus/Vive)
- Photoshop (Texturing)
- Substance Painter (Materials)

**Key Outcomes:**
- Addressed community concerns through realistic visualization
- Enabled stakeholders to experience the proposed changes at 1:1 scale
- Facilitated informed decision-making for expansion project
- Demonstrated neighborhood impact before construction began

**Impact:** Helped community members and decision-makers visualize and understand the real-world impact of the proposed expansion in an immersive, accurate way.

**Category Tags:** VR, Architectural Visualization, Community Planning

## Project 3: ADHD Empathy Tool for Parent Education

**Category:** VR Healthcare / Medical Training

**Client:** Psychiatrists treating children with ADHD

**Challenge:** Parents of children with ADHD struggled to understand what their children were experiencing, making it difficult to empathize and provide appropriate support.

**Solution:** Developed an immersive VR empathy tool that simulates the ADHD experience, allowing parents to:
- Experience sensory overload and distractibility
- Understand attention challenges their children face
- Gain deeper empathy through first-hand virtual experience
- Better support their children's needs

**Technologies:**
- Unity (VR Development)
- VR Headsets
- Audio/Visual Effects Programming
- Interaction Design for Medical Applications

**Key Outcomes:**
- Improved parent understanding of ADHD experiences
- Enhanced empathy and support for affected children
- Valuable tool for psychiatrists in parent education
- Demonstrated VR's potential in medical empathy training

**Impact:** Helped bridge the empathy gap between parents and children with ADHD by providing experiential understanding rather than just intellectual knowledge.

**Category Tags:** VR, Healthcare, Medical Training, Empathy Tool
