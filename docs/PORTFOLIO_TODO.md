## Recommended Portfolio Size

### Industry Best Practice: **4-5 Featured Projects**

**Why 4-5 is ideal:**

✅ **Pros of 4-5 projects:**
- Shows breadth of experience across service types
- Demonstrates versatility (different industries)
- Enough variety without overwhelming visitors
- Allows one project per main service category
- Provides strong social proof
- Easy to scan and digest on landing page
- Industry standard for B2B service websites

❌ **Why not fewer (1-2)?**
- Looks like limited experience
- Single failure point (if client relationship sours)
- Doesn't show range of capabilities
- Harder to connect with diverse prospects

❌ **Why not more (6+)?**
- Overwhelming for first-time visitors
- Takes too long to review on main page
- Better suited for dedicated "/portfolio" page
- Dilutes impact of best work
- Analysis paralysis for potential clients
